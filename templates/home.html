About The Orchard

Welcome to Couch Pecan Orchard, a cherished family-owned destination on Route 66 in Luther, Oklahoma. Owned by the Stroud family, we are committed to preserving our orchard's iconic status and providing a memorable experience. From the annual Pecan Festival celebrating local traditions to our thriving groves filled with the finest pecans, Couch Pecan Orchard is where history and flavor intertwine. Immerse yourself in our enchanting ambiance, indulge in the irresistible taste of handpicked pecans, and join us in celebrating the charm of Route 66. Welcome to a truly unique journey along the historic route.
